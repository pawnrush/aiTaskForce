From Data Overload to Defensible Drafts

The Challenge: Synthesizing assessment data and observations into a compliant "Present Levels of Academic Achievement and Functional Performance" (PLAAFP) section.

AI Assist Prompt:
"Summarize the key findings from these sources for a 4th-grade student's reading fluency and comprehension. Highlight areas of strength and need."

Connecting Dots for Positive Interventions

The Challenge: Translating FBA data into function-based, positive replacement behaviors and strategies.

AI Assist Prompt:
"Based on the attached FBA which identifies the function of behavior as escape from non-preferred tasks, generate a list of 5 potential replacement behaviors and 5 antecedent strategies."

From Parent Emails to Meeting Summaries

The Challenge: Spending hours on routine communication and administrative tasks.

AI Assist Prompt:
"Based on the IEP goals source, draft a professional, positive email to a parent summarizing their child's progress in second nine weeks grading period."

From One Lesson to Many Paths

The Challenge: Quickly creating materials for a wide range of academic and social-emotional needs.

AI Assist Prompt:
"Using the attached science text on photosynthesis, create a vocabulary list with simple definitions for a 3rd-grade level. Then, suggest 3 hands-on activities to reinforce the concepts."

Workflow in Action: Prompt Chaining

Prompt chaining is using the output from one prompt to build the next. This creates a powerful, multi-step workflow where you guide the AI from raw data to a finished product.

Ethics & Responsibility: Your Human Guardrails

Using AI ethically is paramount. Your professional judgment and commitment to student privacy are non-negotiable.

De-Identification is Mandatory

You must NEVER upload Personally Identifiable Information (PII). Remove all identifying data before uploading any document.

BEFORE

Report for John Doe, DOB 5/15/2015. FBA conducted by Ms. Smith.

AFTER

Report for [Student], DOB [DOB]. FBA conducted by [Teacher].
